What is Viewly Alpha?

Viewly Alpha is a tokenized video platform, aiming to provide a more fair and efficient means of content delivery and monetization. Alpha is built with cool tech like Ethereum and IPFS. Alpha is in an experimental phase, and as such is not for the faint of heart.
