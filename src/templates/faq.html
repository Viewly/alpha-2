Frequently Asked Questions

We'd like to thank everyone helping in expansion of this FAQ. If you don't find an answer you're looking for please reach out to us here. We are looking forward answering your questions.

Viewly

What is Viewly Alpha?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

Viewly Alpha is an experimental video platform, aiming to provide a more fair and efficient means of content delivery and monetization. We are trying out modern, decentralized technologies such as Ethereum and IPFS.

Why are you doing this?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

We would like to help niche content creators connect with their viewers and receive rewards for their hard work. We also dislike ads, just as much as you do.

Where can I find the source code?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

You can find the source of the underlying Ethereum contracts here. The IPFS infrastructure and other tools will be open-sourced and published on our GitHub later this year.

I have some feedback. How can I contact you?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

If you have a product idea, feature request, or constructive criticism - we would love to hear it. Constructive feedback will be rewarded with VIEW Tokens.

Blockchain

What is Ethereum?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

Ethereum, the blockchain, is an open software platform based on blockchain technology that enables developers to build and deploy decentralized applications. On Ethereum every transaction uses ETH in the form of Gas and Gwei. For more information, please refer to the following article.

What is a VIEW token?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

The VIEW token is a utility token that functions as a medium of exchange between users activity and publishers who participate in the Viewly ecosystem. It's used by token owners, people who hold VIEW tokens, in the Viewly ecosystem to reward content creators for their hard work by voting on their videos and by content creators, which are also token owners, to publish their videos. VIEW token is an ERC-20 compliant token on the Ethereum blockchain.

Where can I see how much a token is worth?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

Yes, although we can't control market conditions, VIEW tokens are being traded on secondary markets, more info here.

Do you have access the funds in my wallet?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

The seed words and the private keys are generated client side. We store an encrypted backup only. If you forget your encryption password or the seed mnemonic, your funds will be unrecoverable.

Rewards

What is the distribution game?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

The stake based game (Viewly Creator Rewards) is a content subsidy program, in which creators who publish high quality content compete for votes from token owners. The videos are rewarded with freshly minted VIEW Tokens in proportion to the cumulative stake of votes relative to all videos within a distribution period.

What is the maximum reward my video can earn?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

The maximum reward is determined by the daily rewards pool.

What is a daily rewards pool?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

A reward period is a 24 hour window which binds the videos published within said window to its respective rewards pool. During the first 7 days, all of the videos from that period compete to receive rewards from the daily pool. You can always check how rewards are distributed by accessing the following link.

How is the daily rewards pool set?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

The rewards pool is based on a combination of how many videos are published and how many votes are cast in recent days.

What happens after my video has been live for 7 days?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

After the 7 days are up, the rewards for your video will be calculated and sent in the next distribution period. Your video stops earning VIEW tokens. You will be able to monetize your video even further as we release other monetization options ( for example tipping, subscriptions, etc.).

Will I be receiving an email when I get my rewards?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

Yes, an email is automatically sent as soon as the rewards are distributed. After the 7 day voting accumulation period has passed ( 7 days after your video has been published), we aim to distribute all rewards once a week, although sometimes it might take longer, but no more than 2 weeks.

How can I turn my VIEW tokens into real money?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

You can use an exchange. A list of exchanges can be found here.

What is "When rewards" and where can I see more information on the tokens and votes I received?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

When over 7 days since publishing your video have passed you will see instead of number of votes how many tokens you have received.

"When rewards" might also represent that the weight of the votes that your video received was too low compared to the total weight of votes in that period.

As a rule of thumb if "when rewards" is seen on the page, in order to see more information, you will have to click on either "when rewards" or the number of tokens (in case they are shown). Alternatively, you can also find more information by searching for your video ID on the following page.

Where can I learn more about the game?

{% include 'atoms/icon-chevron-down.html' %}

Please check the distribution game.
